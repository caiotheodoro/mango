# Brazilian Mango Export Data

## Production Overview (2023)
- **Total Production**: Approximately 1.4 million metric tons
- **Export Volume**: 250,000+ metric tons
- **Export Value**: US$ 280 million
- **World Ranking**: 7th largest mango producer, 4th largest exporter
- **Export Season**: September to March (counter-seasonal to Northern Hemisphere)

## Top Export Destinations (2023)

### 1. European Union (65% of exports)
- **Netherlands**: Primary entry point, distribution hub (35%)
- **United Kingdom**: 12%
- **Portugal**: 8%
- **Spain**: 5%
- **Germany**: 5%

### 2. United States (25% of exports)
- Second largest market
- Growing demand for organic mangos
- Primary ports: Miami, Los Angeles

### 3. Other Markets (10%)
- Canada
- Middle East (UAE, Saudi Arabia)
- Asia (Japan, South Korea)

## Competitive Advantages

### Counter-Seasonal Supply
Brazil's harvest (Sep-Mar) perfectly complements:
- India/Pakistan (Apr-Jul)
- Mexico (Mar-Sep)
- Peru (Dec-Feb, direct competitor)

### Geographic Positioning
- Vale do São Francisco: 9°S latitude
- Year-round irrigation from São Francisco River
- Controlled flowering through irrigation management

### Infrastructure
- **Petrolina Airport**: Direct cargo flights to Europe
- **Port of Salvador**: Maritime exports
- **Cold Chain**: Advanced packing houses with GLOBALGAP certification

## Certifications and Standards
- **GLOBALGAP**: Required for EU exports
- **GRASP**: Social practices certification
- **Organic Certification**: Growing segment (5% of exports)
- **Rainforest Alliance**: Environmental certification
- **Fair Trade**: Social certification option
